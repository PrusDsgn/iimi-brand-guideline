Brand Headline Guidelines

Option 01 - Primary

Your data should be
working for you.

We get it. Most organizations are sitting on information they can't use - and it's costing them every day.

Option 02 - Problem-first

Buried, not gone.

Your records exist. They just can't be found, used, or trusted. We change that.

Option 03 - AI-forward

Before the AI,
there is the data.

Every AI initiative starts with clean, structured, accessible data. IIMI builds that foundation.

Voice & Tone

How IIMI sounds. Always direct. Never corporate.

→

Direct

State the problem. State the solution. No fluff.

Do

"Your data should be working for you."

Don't

"We provide innovative data management solutions."

◎

Human

Talk to the person, not the enterprise. They feel the pain.

Do

"Right now someone on your team is wasting time."

Don't

"Organizations face challenges with data utilization."

✓

Confident

28 years. 7,200 people. Proven track record. Say it plainly.

Do

"We've been doing this since 1996."

Don't

"We're proud to be a trusted leader in the space."

Headline Formulas

Four patterns that work for IIMI. Use these as starting points.

01Problem → Relief

"Files buried for years. Searchable by Monday."

State the pain. Resolve it with a timeframe.

02Observation → Implication

"Your team is doing this manually. They shouldn't be."

Acknowledge what they're already doing. Challenge it.

03Honest question

"Is your data ready for what comes next?"

No lecture. Just ask the question they're already asking themselves.

04Understatement + precision

"One error. Every 20,000 records."

Confidence through specificity. Let the number speak.
